About

I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & IT, Kathua Campus, University of Jammu. My teaching and research focus on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Mining, Time Series Forecasting, Healthcare Analytics, Medical Imaging, and ICT-enabled education.

My doctoral work, titled “Knowledge Discovery in Maternal Health and Child Immunization Databases”, explored data mining and ensemble learning approaches for maternal and child health decision support.

Over the years, I have contributed extensively to research in public healthcare analytics, COVID-19 time series forecasting, deep learning for medical imaging, and ensemble methods for disease diagnosis and prediction, along with active involvement in academic administration, outreach, and capacity building programmes.
