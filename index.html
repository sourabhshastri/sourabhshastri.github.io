About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & IT, Kathua Campus, University of Jammu. My teaching and research interests span Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Healthcare Analytics, Medical Imaging, and Data Mining.

My Ph.D. research focused on knowledge discovery in large-scale maternal health and child immunization databases using advanced data mining and ensemble learning approaches.
